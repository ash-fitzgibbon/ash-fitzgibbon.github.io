What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence refers to a persons ability to understand, control and evaluate emotions. It is often considered to
be a skill that can be learned and strengthened.The ability to express and control your own emotions is critical but that
alone doesn't correlate to a high emotional intelligence. You have to be able to understand,interpret and develop emotional
responses to the people around you.


How is it different to iq?

Put simply IQ is a measure of your ability to problem solve , apply logic and to grasp and convey complex ideas. Emotional
intelligence measures how well you are able to recognize emotion in yourself and others and then how well you use that
awareness to make decisions.

Why is it important?

I think its important as it is something that you can apply to pretty much every relationship you have in your life. In work
environments it allows you to understand your co-workers thoughts and decisions better as well as allow you to apply yourself
correctly in a group context leading to being a more productive colleague and worker in collaborative environments. In personal
relationships ie partner or friends a high Emotional intelligence allows you better to be able to understand them and help respond
to problems they are facing in their lives better aswell as allowing you to be able to convey your emotions to them in a more productive way.
