The difference between Padding,Border and Margin

  • Border:Acts as a sort of boundary that surrounds the element but also goes on the outside of the padding. When made visible it makes it easy to visualize
    your different elements.
  • Margin: A margin is the space that surrounds an element and seperates them from eachother.
  • Padding:is the space between the element and the related content inside of it. This changes how elements within a container look and fit with eachother.
    When you increase the padding either the element has to grow bigger of the content inside has to shrink , by default the element increases in size.
