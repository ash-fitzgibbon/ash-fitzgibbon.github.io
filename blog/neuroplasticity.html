Research Growth Mindset
Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

Growth mindset is how we think when learning new things especially when faced with challenges. It is when people approach learning with a mindset of even if they have difficulty now its not because of innate "talent" or anything like that its just because there skill isn't there YET. It tells us that with work and effort we can improve at things over time. It is relevant to EDA due to the fact that save for a few of us that have CS degree backgrounds coming in to the course , for most people this is a course that is full of new information and skills we did not have prior to joining.


In this exploration, did anything surprise you?

Not really as growth mindset was something I was already aware of and had discussed with my brother at length.


Change for you?

I will say that seeing things like this is a reminder in how to approach my learning and remember the benefits of growth mindset.


How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

I will use it to help me problem solve when faced with difficulty as like I said a large percentage of what we are learning and doing week to week is completely new for me.
